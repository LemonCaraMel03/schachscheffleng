    Chess School

    Tuerm a Sprénger Matt

    Our training sessions are open to everyone, ages 5 to 99 and over. The benefits of chess have been proven by numerous studies.

    • Improved cognitive skills, such as attention, memory and logical thinking, which are essential to a person's development.
    • More creativity, enhanced by problem-solving.
    • Critical thinking reinforces one's ability to distinguish between the strengths and weaknesses of each party and thus can help to make appropriate judgements and decisions.
    • A sense of ethics, which often leads to an improvement in the behaviour of young players.
    • Improved language skills.

    Our Club

    The chess club Tuerm a Sprénger Matt Schëffleng a.s.b.l was founded on the 22 May 2003 through when the "Cercle d'échecs Tour et Cavalier Belvaux" merged with the "Cercle d'échecs Matt Schifflange".

    • Today, our club is one of the biggest and best chess clubs in Luxembourg.
    • Our first team plays in the national division and our chess school is known beyond the borders of our municipality and even beyond the borders of the country.
